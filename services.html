Ad Formats

Supported Advertisement Types

Our mobile applications support a variety of ad formats to optimize user engagement and revenue generation.

Splash Ads

Full-screen advertisements displayed during app launch. These ads capture user attention immediately and are designed to be visually appealing while loading the main content.

Rewarded Video Ads

User-initiated video advertisements that provide rewards such as in-app currency, premium features, or additional content. These ads offer high engagement rates and positive user experience.

Interstitial Ads

Full-screen ads that appear at natural transition points in the app, such as between levels or after completing a task. These ads have high visibility and click-through rates.

Banner Ads

Standard rectangular advertisements displayed at the top or bottom of the screen. These non-intrusive ads provide steady revenue while maintaining app usability.
