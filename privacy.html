5. Advertising and Ad Monetization

5.1 Overview

We monetize our mobile applications through advertising. Our apps integrate multiple advertising platforms and networks to display advertisements. This section explains how we use advertising technologies and the information collected through advertising.

5.2 Advertising Platforms We Use

Our applications integrate the following advertising platforms and networks:

  • Google AdMob - Google's mobile advertising platform
  • Facebook Audience Network - Facebook's mobile advertising network
  • Unity Ads - Unity Technologies' advertising platform
  • AppLovin - Mobile advertising and monetization platform
  • IronSource - Mobile advertising and app monetization platform
  • MoPub (Twitter) - Twitter's mobile ad mediation platform
  • Chartboost - Mobile game advertising and monetization platform
  • Vungle - Mobile video advertising platform
  • Mintegral - Global mobile advertising platform
  • 穿山甲 (Pangle) - ByteDance's advertising platform
  • Smaato - Global mobile advertising exchange
  • OpenX - Programmatic advertising exchange
  • InMobi - Mobile advertising and monetization platform
  • Mobvista - Global mobile marketing platform
  • Liftoff - Mobile app marketing and engagement platform

5.3 Types of Advertisements

Our applications may display the following types of advertisements:

  • Splash Ads: Full-screen advertisements displayed during app launch or loading
  • Rewarded Video Ads: Video advertisements that users can watch in exchange for in-app rewards
  • Interstitial Ads: Full-screen advertisements displayed at natural transition points
  • Banner Ads: Rectangular display advertisements shown at the top or bottom of the screen
  • Native Ads: Advertisements designed to match the look and feel of the app content
  • Playable Ads: Interactive advertisements that allow users to preview app content

5.4 Information Collected for Advertising

Advertising platforms may collect and use:

  • Device identifiers (GAID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
  • Device information (type, model, operating system)
  • Location data (country, region, city)
  • App usage data and behavioral patterns
  • IP address
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Cookie data (for web-based ads)
  • Conversion tracking data

5.5 AdMob Specific Information

When you use apps with Google AdMob:

  • Google may collect and process data as described in Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • You can control interest-based advertising through Google's Ads Settings
  • AdMob may use collected data for personalization of ads based on your interests
  • Google provides options to opt out of personalized advertising

5.6 How to Control Advertising

You can control advertising preferences in the following ways:

  • Device Settings: On Android, you can reset or opt out of advertising ID through device settings. On iOS, you can enable "Limit Ad Tracking" in Privacy settings.
  • Platform-Specific Settings:
    • Google: https://adssettings.google.com
    • Apple: Enable Limit Ad Tracking in iOS Settings > Privacy > Advertising
  • Within Our Apps: Some of our apps may offer in-app settings to manage ad preferences

5.7 Ad Revenue and Analytics

We use analytics tools to measure advertising performance and optimize ad placement. This includes tracking:

  • Ad impression counts
  • Click-through rates
  • Conversion rates
  • Revenue generated from ads
  • User engagement with ads

  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings

6.3 Third-Party Cookies

6.4 Managing Cookies

  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request limitation of processing
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request transfer of your data
  • Right to Object: You can object to certain processing activities
